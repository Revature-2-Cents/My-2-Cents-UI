The 50 - 30 - 20 Budgeting Rule


This budget calculator is based on the 50 - 30 - 20 rule of thumb. Essentially, this rule breaks down your income into percentages that represent categories. Your Needs are equal to 50% percent of your total income. Needs qualify as things you cannot live without like: groceries, mortgage, insurance. Your Wants are equal to 30 percent of your income and would be eating out, hobbies, vacation, or shopping. Finally, Savings is equal to 20 percent of your income and represents items such as your 401K, mutual funds, index funds, or other related holdings.
